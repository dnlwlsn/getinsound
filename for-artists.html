Artist FAQ

Everything you'd ask.

Is the 90% rate permanent?+

Yes. 90% is not a launch offer and not subject to change. Insound is bootstrapped with no investors, so there's no cap table that could push the rate up later.

Does Insound hold my money?+

Never. 90% goes directly to your Stripe account the moment a sale completes. Withdrawals to your bank follow Stripe's standard payout schedule.

What formats do you accept?+

WAV, FLAC, AIFF and MP3. Upload your masters and we'll handle preview generation and downloads.

Are there any hidden fees?+

No. Stripe charges 1.5% + 20p per transaction, shown transparently at checkout — passed through at cost, no markup. Insound's 10% is all we take.

Do I keep my masters?+

Always. Uploading to Insound gives us nothing except permission to host and sell your music on your behalf. You own everything, forever.

Is there a subscription fee?+

No. £0 to join, £0 per month, £0 per year. We only make money when you make money.

What happens if I want to leave?+

You can take your music down at any time, free. No exit fees, no lock-in, no takedown charges.

Can I set pay-what-you-want pricing?+

Yes. Every release supports pay-what-you-want with a minimum you set (from £2). Fans can always pay more if they love it.

How do download codes work?+

Generate free download codes for any release — share them with press, playlists, or fans. Each code redeems for a single download. Coming at launch.

Can I do pre-orders?+

Yes. Set a release date and take pre-orders in advance. Fans get notified on release day; you get paid at the point of sale.
