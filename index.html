When does Insound launch?

We're in development now. Founding members get first access before we open to everyone — that's what the waitlist is for.

Is the 90% rate permanent?

Yes. It's not a launch promotion or an introductory offer — it's the whole business model. We keep 10% to cover development, storage, and the team. That's the deal, permanently.

How do I get paid?

90% of every sale hits your balance the moment it completes. Withdraw anytime — no minimum thresholds, no delays.

Do I keep my masters?

Always. Uploading to Insound gives us nothing except permission to host and sell your music on your behalf. You own everything, forever.

What formats do you accept?

WAV, FLAC, AIFF, and MP3. We recommend lossless where possible — your fans deserve the best quality.

Is there a subscription fee?

No. It's free to publish. We only make money when you make money — 10% per sale, nothing else.
