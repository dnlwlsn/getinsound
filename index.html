When does Insound launch?

We're in development now. Founding members get first access before we open to everyone — that's what the waitlist is for.

Is the 90% rate permanent?

Yes. It's not a launch promotion or an introductory offer — it's the whole business model. We keep 10% to cover development, storage, and the team. That's the deal, permanently.

How do I get paid?

90% goes directly to your Stripe account the moment a sale completes — we never hold it. Your Stripe balance is yours immediately. Withdrawals to your bank follow Stripe's standard payout schedule, typically 2–7 days depending on your account settings. No minimum thresholds, no delays on our end.

Does Insound hold my money?

Never. We use Stripe Connect direct charges — when a fan buys your music, the payment is created directly in your Stripe account. We take our 10% as an application fee at the point of sale. Your money is yours from the moment the transaction completes. We are never in the middle.

Do I keep my masters?

Always. Uploading to Insound gives us nothing except permission to host and sell your music on your behalf. You own everything, forever.

What formats do you accept?

WAV, FLAC, AIFF, and MP3. We recommend lossless where possible — your fans deserve the best quality.

Is there a subscription fee?

No. It's free to publish. We only make money when you make money — 10% per sale, nothing else.

Are there any hidden fees?

None. We take 10% per sale. Stripe charges 1.5% + 20p per transaction — shown clearly at checkout and passed through at cost. No markup on the Stripe fee. What you see is what happens.
